Making slaw

Slaw is a favorite of Dwarves and autistic Texans everywhere! Today we will be teaching you how to make it.

Ingredients

  • A pound of raw gleet
  • Two gallons of blood
  • Raw ass
  • Proto-slaw
  • Class-four hasmat suit
  1. Glob the gleet into sleet
  2. Mix the blood into the sleet until it glubbles
  3. Grate raw ass flakes into the mix and stir until well combined
  4. Add in one teaspoon of Proto-slaw to convert the mix into slaw
  5. Put on the hasmat suit before it kills you
  6. You failed. You are now slaw.
